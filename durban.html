DURBAN



Durban (eThekwini) in the Zulu language is the third most popular city in South Africa and the lagest city in the KwaZulu Natal province. It is situated in the east coast of South Africa and id the busiest port in the country. Dueban is famous of its extensive beaches and warm subtropical climate and that makes it one of the major tourism centres. It has a population of about 3.44 million making it one of the biggest cities on the Indian Ocean coast of the African continent. Durban is also the second most important manufacturing hub in South Africa. 2015 it was officially recognised as one of the New 7 Wonder Cities. Durban was one of the host cities for the 2010 FIFA World Cup.



MOSES MABHIDA STADIUM


Moses Mabida Stadium one of the most famous stadiums in the country and it where the matches were held during the FIFA World Cup in 2010. The stadium is not for matches only but there are some activities that take place there and those activities include a Skycar ride or Adventure Walk to the top of the arch with 360 degree view over Durban; Guinnesss world record Bungee swing; Segway gliding tours of the stadium; Cafes and Restaurants. All these activities help boost the economy of the city and the province at large.



USHAKA MARINE WORLD


UShaka Marine World is a 16-hectare theme park which was opened on 13 April 2004 in Durban.It has 8 sections:

  1. uShaka Sea World
  2. uShaka Wet 'n Wild
  3. uShaka Sea Animal Encounter Island
  4. uShaka Beach
  5. uShaka Village Walk
  6. uShaka Kids Wold
  7. uShaka Dangerous Creatures
  8. Chimp & Zee

UShaka Marine World incorporates fresh and sea water, lush vegetaion, natural materials and the re-creation of a wreck of a 1940s cargo ship. UShaka is the 5th largesr aquarium in the world. The park is tastefully themed with a focus on family entertainment fit for every occassion.

Other venues that people like to visit are:

  • Kingsmead Cricket Ground - is a major test match and none-day cricket venue.
  • Kings Park Staduim - is the host to the internationally reowned Sharks Rugby Team.
  • Greyville Racecourse - home of Durban July Handicap and Durban Country Club and golf course.
  • Durban Ice Arena - activies include leisure ice skating, birthday parties, school excursions, sporting events, teamingbuilding activities, corporate functions and group bookings.
